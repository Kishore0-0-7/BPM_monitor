How to use:

1. Make sure your face is clearly visible in the video.

2. Stay relatively still while the system calculates your heart rate.

3. Ensure you have good lighting on your face.

4. Wait for approximately 10 seconds for an initial reading.

About:

This application uses Eulerian Video Magnification to detect subtle color changes in your face that correspond to your heart rate.

Note: This is an experimental tool and should not be used for medical purposes.
